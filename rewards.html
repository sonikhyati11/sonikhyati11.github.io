Salary Rewards

What is Salary rewards?

Salary rewards is a module by which any salary account holder can earn rewards every month on receiving salary. It is the only module that exists in India.

The goal was to create a UI with gamification for user engagement.

Journey

Due to unavailability of relevant references, It was challenging to design a module which incorporate all the features and type of interactions that user would look upon for opting for a salary account. We did many rounds of discussion around specs with PMs and did research around how people operate the salary account and their expectations.

  • The first and important thing was to design the entrypoint and discoverability of this feature.
  • As we already had one CTA for rewards, keeping the new feature on the second level was not a suitable option, thus we decided to put another CTA on the homepage to drive more users to explore this feature. We also kept a tertiary entry point from the rewards module for explorations.
  • We created a happy flow and started analyzing all the errors and corner cases.
  • After many iterations and stakeholders reviews we came up with an engaging and appealing UI.
