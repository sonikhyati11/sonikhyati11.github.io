User Onboarding

What is Onboarding?

The Journey of a user from the app download to digital bank account creation is called Onboarding. The Goal of the project was to bring down onboarding time to less than 100 seconds!

Challenges

Before making the ‚Äòwhat‚Äô, we need to know the ‚Äòwhy‚Äô. Hence, I started by learning the need for onboarding.

The Bank account creation requires detailed personal information as well documentation. Getting all these information from user in a easy to follow and smaller flow was one of the main challenge along with it also need to make sure to design which builds the sense of trust and authenticity.

Before we started this project, we already had a working flow which was taking around 5 minutes for onboarding. After analyzing the data to Increasing the User Retention Business team came up with the hypothesis that the drops are because of the lengthy onboarding process. So the goal was to bring down the total time for onboarding to less than 100 seconds, and we picked up the 1 minute onboaridng challenge. Journey

After observing the data we tried to make the belwo mentioned changes to the flow.

  • Identifying redundant fields
  • Populating information via Aadhaar or PAN verification
  • Reducing clicks by auto submitting the forms
  • Adding timer to view only screens to avoid CTA

After incorporating the above mentioned points, we created a high level prototype for user testing. We refined the workflow further based on the feedback received from user testing.

Result

  • We were able to reduce the clicks from 36 to 24 for the happy flow and we are able to maintain a conversion of healthy 25%.
  • Total average time of onboarding was reduced from 5 minutes to 2 minutes
